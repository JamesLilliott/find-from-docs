Join Our Team

We are always on the lookout for talented and passionate individuals who share our love of biking and our commitment to excellent customer service. If you are looking for an exciting career in the bike industry, we invite you to explore the opportunities available at our bike shop.

Current Openings

Sales Associate: We are seeking a sales associate to join our team. The ideal candidate should have a passion for cycling, strong communication skills, and the ability to provide excellent customer service. Sales experience is preferred but not required.

Bicycle Technician: We are seeking a bicycle technician to join our team. The ideal candidate should have experience with bike repair and maintenance, a strong attention to detail, and the ability to work independently. Certification from a recognized bike mechanics program is preferred.

Store Manager: We are seeking a store manager to oversee the daily operations of our bike shop. The ideal candidate should have experience in retail management, a passion for cycling, and the ability to lead a team. Excellent communication and organizational skills are required.

Benefits

We offer our employees competitive wages, a supportive work environment, and opportunities for career advancement. We also offer a generous employee discount on bikes, accessories, and services.

How to Apply

If you are interested in joining our team, please send your resume and cover letter to james@webikes.com. We thank all applicants for their interest, but only those selected for an interview will be contacted.

We look forward to hearing from you!
